# MIT - Machine Learning Systems Faculty

## Research Focus: Machine Learning Systems (MLSys)

This list includes faculty at MIT who research topics related to machine learning systems, including distributed training, ML frameworks, efficient inference, AI infrastructure, and systems for ML.
