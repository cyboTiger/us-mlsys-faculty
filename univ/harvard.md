# Harvard University - Machine Learning Systems Faculty

## Research Focus: Machine Learning Systems (MLSys)

This list includes faculty at Harvard University who research topics related to machine learning systems, including distributed training, ML frameworks, efficient inference, AI infrastructure, and systems for ML.

---

## Faculty

### Vijay Reddi
- **Title**: Gordon McKay Professor of Electrical Engineering
- **Research**: Computer architecture, machine learning systems, edge AI, autonomous agents
- **Website**: https://salatainstitute.harvard.edu/faculty/vijay-reddi/

### Sham Kakade
- **Title**: Rampell Family Professor of Computer Science and Professor of Statistics, Co-director of Kempner Institute
- **Research**: Foundations of machine learning, distributed training, foundation models, optimization
- **Website**: https://shamulent.github.io/

### David Alvarez-Melis
- **Title**: Assistant Professor of Computer Science (SEAS)
- **Research**: Data-centric machine learning, AI reliability, ML systems
- **Website**: https://dmelis.github.io/

### Finale Doshi-Velez
- **Title**: Professor of Computer Science
- **Research**: Probabilistic modeling, human-AI interaction, healthcare ML
- **Website**: https://finale.seas.harvard.edu/

### Kunle Olukotun
- **Title**: Professor of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science
- **Research**: Parallel computing, ML hardware, domain-specific architectures
- **Website**: https://www.seas.harvard.edu/faculty/kunle-olukotun
